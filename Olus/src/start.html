Why Olus
The browser most people actually need — without the weight, the tracking, or the lock-in.
🗂️

Never lose your tabs ★

Every tab is saved to disk the moment it changes and restored on launch — survives crashes, updates and restarts. Point the session at a synced folder and it follows you across machines.

🪶

Featherweight

Built on the system Chromium (WebView2) engine — nothing to bundle. The app is a few megabytes and sips RAM, where Chrome ships a ~150 MB engine per install.

🌐

Browse from anywhere

Route your traffic through Tor or any country's proxy with one dropdown. Look like you're somewhere else — or stay private — without extensions.

🔑

Sign in like normal

Real Chrome user-agent and a persistent profile, so Google, GitHub and the rest let you sign in and stay signed in — no "insecure browser" walls.

✦

AI sidebar built in

Pop open an AI assistant next to any page with one click (or Ctrl+J). Ask about what you're reading without leaving the tab.

🎨

Themes & yours to bend

A clean Microsoft-blue light theme or a focused dark one — your call. Open and tiny, so a reader mode or ad blocker is a few lines away.

</>

Dev tools built in

A bottom dock with the real Chromium DevTools (console, network, errors), a terminal, a REST/API client, and a one-click localhost server — ship without leaving the browser. (Ctrl+`)
