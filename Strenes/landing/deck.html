The gap

Every filter today serves the platform.
None serve the person being messaged.

  • 41% of Americans have experienced online harassment (Pew Research) — messaging apps ship zero receiver-side protection.
  • Elder scams arrive over SMS and chat; the target is the person least equipped to spot them.
  • Creators and professionals drown in unsolicited DMs with no way to say "only show me what matters."
  • Platform moderation optimizes for the platform's liability — not for your boundaries, your family, your quiet hours.
The concept

Your inbox, defended by your AI — running on your device.

Incoming messages are classified on-device (abusive / spam / business / promo / clean) and routed by rules the user states in plain language. No message content leaves the phone for moderation. The AI answers to the receiver — not to a platform.

What's in the build today

🛡️ On-device AI filter
Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano + a rules engine fallback. Classifies and routes every incoming message locally.
🧒 Guardian mode
Kid-safe phone: grooming patterns, photo requests, meet-up pressure and kid-targeted scams blocked on-device — with an instant alert to the linked parent that never exposes the message content.
🤖 Commander
Natural-language inbox assistant: briefings, free-form rules, mutes, protection profiles, memory, busy windows.
🔐 Encrypted DMs & groups
ECDH P-256 + AES-256-GCM, private keys never leave the device. WhatsApp-style groups with per-member key wrapping.
📞 Voice calls
Real WebRTC calling over the same relay. Accept/decline UI on any screen.
📅 Google Calendar
"Schedule a meeting with Maya tomorrow 3pm" — Commander books it (OAuth API or prefilled event).
📱 Ships everywhere
Installable PWA, signed-ready Android APK via CI, iOS Xcode project. Supabase backend with RLS.

Where a funded team takes this

Three wedges, one core asset.

  • Teen & family safety — Guardian mode is already in the build: grooming detection + parent alerts. Parents already pay for protection; this is the visceral pitch. (COPPA + parental dashboard next.)
  • Elder scam shield — a protection profile already exists in the build; the demographic loses billions/yr to message scams.
  • B2B SDK — license the on-device filtering layer to dating apps, creator platforms, and workplaces that can't send content to a cloud.
  • Roadmap in repo: Signal-protocol upgrade, push notifications, Play Store release, verified OTP auth.
The biggest buyer isn't a consumer

Citizen protection as public infrastructure.

Governments across Europe are under pressure to protect children online (UK Online Safety Act, EU DSA, chat-control debates) — but every proposal collides with privacy. Strenes dissolves that conflict: the AI runs on the citizen's device. No scanning in the cloud, no backdoor, no data collection. Protection without surveillance.

  • GDPR-native by architecture — message content never leaves the phone; there is nothing to subpoena, leak, or breach.
  • National kid-safety layer — Guardian mode as a state-endorsed app for families: grooming and predator patterns blocked on-device, parents alerted.
  • Elder scam defense at population scale — the same engine, one profile switch. Message scams cost EU citizens billions annually.
  • White-label handoff — a ministry or public agency can take this codebase, brand it, localize the threat lexicons, and ship it to their people.
