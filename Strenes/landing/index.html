Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for users who want control, privacy, and intelligence in their inbox — all running locally on your device.

🤖
Commander — AI inbox assistant

Chat with your inbox. "Reply Maya yes I'll be there", "open Alex", "show held". Commander briefs you on every unread sender in streaming AI bubbles, then takes action on your command.

🛡️
On-device AI moderation

Gemini Nano runs entirely on your hardware via Chrome's Prompt API. Message text never leaves your device. RulesModerator provides an instant fallback on unsupported browsers.

📂
Smart inbox — 4 folders

Primary for real conversations, Business for OTPs and receipts, Promotions for offers, Review for borderline messages. Everything lands where it belongs, automatically.

⏰
Do Not Disturb + Disappearing

Set quiet hours — DND silences everyone except trusted and emergency contacts. Disappearing messages vanish after a timer: on read, 1 min, 1 hour, 24 hours. Countdown lives in the bubble.

🍺
Drunk mode detection

Analyzes typing speed, caps lock ratio, and typo density. When you seem impaired, it warns you or blocks sending entirely — your future self will thank you.

🧠
Tone analyzer + spell-check

Tone analysis catches aggressive or anxiety-inducing drafts before you send. Style-aware spell-check learns your writing voice and suggests corrections that sound like you.

How it works

Three steps. Inbox peace.

From message sent to message seen — the full pipeline runs on your device. Nothing ever touches a server.

1
Message arrives

A message comes in. Before it touches your inbox, Strenes intercepts it on-device — no server round-trip, no latency, no exposure.

2
AI classifies it

Gemini Nano (or the rules engine fallback) classifies the message as clean, spam, abusive, business, or promotional — in milliseconds.

3
Routed your way

Clean lands in Primary. Business goes to Business. Abuse is blocked. Borderline waits in Review, blurred, for you to decide — on your terms.
