Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don’t.

Seven pillars that make Sift the most advanced message filter ever built for a PWA.

🛡️
On-device AI moderation

Powered by Gemini Nano via Chrome’s built-in Prompt API. The model runs entirely on your hardware. Message text never leaves — not even to us. RulesModerator kicks in as a silent fallback on unsupported browsers.

📂
Four smart folders

Primary for personal conversations, Business for OTPs and delivery updates, Promotions for offers, and Review for anything borderline. Every message lands exactly where it belongs.

🎛️
Sensitivity dial

Low, Medium, or High — you decide how aggressive the filter is. Adjust per mood, per situation. Never too strict, never too lax, always exactly yours.

🤝
Trusted contact bypass

Mark anyone as trusted. Their messages skip the AI entirely and land straight in Primary — because some people always deserve a direct line, no questions asked.

🫣
Blur & reveal

Filtered messages wait in Review, blurred until you choose. Auto-reject sends a polite refusal to the sender. You see only what you decide to see, on your timeline.

🧪
Filter simulator

Type any message and watch Sift classify it live — category, confidence score, folder destination, and what the sender would see. Tune your rules with real-time feedback.

📞
In-app calling

Call contacts directly from any conversation. A full glassmorphism overlay shows ringing animations, a live call timer, and mute & speaker controls — all without leaving Sift or touching a third-party service.

How it works

Three steps to inbox peace.

From message sent to message seen — Sift’s full pipeline runs entirely on your device.

1
Message arrives

A message comes in. Before it touches your inbox, Sift intercepts it on-device — no server round-trip, no latency, no exposure.

2
AI classifies it

Gemini Nano (or the rules engine as fallback) classifies the message as clean, spam, abusive, business, or promotional — in milliseconds.

3
Routed your way

Clean lands in Primary. Business goes to Business. Abuse is blocked. Borderline waits in Review, blurred, for you to decide — on your terms.
