The Perch promise

  • Message content never leaves the phone. The scanner runs inside the device; there is no code path that uploads a message. Only a flag crosses the wire: category, reason, app, sender name, time.
  • Your kid is in on it. Perch's setup runs on their phone, explains itself in kid language, and shows them every flag it has ever raised — the same list you see. Protection, not spying.
  • Self-harm flags help friends too. If a contact messages your kid about wanting to hurt themselves, you'll know someone in their circle may need an adult.
  • Open source. Every line — the detector, the relay, this page — is public. Verify the promise yourself.

What Perch watches for

🚨

Grooming & secrecy

“Don't tell your parents”, “our little secret”, “are you alone?” — the patterns predators actually use, including l33t-speak obfuscation.

📸

Photo requests

Requests for pictures, camera access, or “what are you wearing” from any contact.

📍

Meet-up pressure

“I'll pick you up”, “come to my place”, “don't bring anyone” — flagged instantly.

🎁

Gift lures

Free Robux, gift cards, cash offers — the classic grooming opener, caught at message one.

💢

Bullying

Targeted insults and exclusion from group chats show up in your weekly digest before they become a crisis.

🆘

Self-harm signals

When someone messages your kid about wanting to hurt themselves, an adult finds out in time.

🎣

Scams

Fake prizes, OTP phishing, account threats — filtered from day one, for kids and grandparents alike.
