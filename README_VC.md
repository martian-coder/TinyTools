## 🎯 What is Strenes?

Strenes is an intelligent message filter that runs entirely on your phone. Every incoming message is analyzed by AI *before it reaches you* — blocking abuse, sorting noise, and letting what matters through.

**The key difference:** Your message text **never leaves your device**. Not even to us.

### Problem We Solve

Every major messaging app (WhatsApp, iMessage, Signal, Telegram) treats all messages equally. You get:
- 😠 Harassment and abuse — unfiltered, unwarned
- 📢 Spam and noise — competing with real messages
- 🔓 Privacy concerns — cloud-based filters see your text
- 🤷 Tone confusion — you can't tell if a message will upset you
- 🌙 Late-night chaos — no way to silence except turning off notifications

**Strenes fixes this** with on-device AI that:
- ✅ Blocks abusive messages before you see them
- ✅ Routes business & promotional mail automatically
- ✅ Analyzes tone and warns of misunderstandings
- ✅ Detects drunk typing patterns
- ✅ Silences notifications on schedule
- ✅ Never stores your message text
- ✅ Never tracks you

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

**Zero cloud. Zero data collection.**

### What Strenes DON'T Do
- ✗ Send messages to our servers
- ✗ Store message content
- ✗ Track or profile you
- ✗ Sell your data
- ✗ Show ads
- ✗ Require signup

### What Strenes DOES Do
- ✅ Run Gemini Nano AI on your phone (offline after first download)
- ✅ Encrypt settings locally
- ✅ Respect your device's security
- ✅ Let you export/delete all data anytime

## 🏗️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
- **AI Model:** Gemini Nano (on-device, no cloud)
- **State:** Zustand v5
- **Mobile:** Capacitor (single codebase, iOS/Android)
- **Infrastructure:** GitHub Pages (stateless; doesn't process messages)

**Zero external API calls for message processing.**
